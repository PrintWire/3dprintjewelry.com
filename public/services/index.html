What We Cover

🕯️

Castable Wax Models

SLA-printed castable resin patterns with zero-ash burnout at 750°C. Exact shrinkage compensation built in. Works with your caster or our in-house casting service.

⚙️

Direct Metal Printing

18K yellow, white, and rose gold; sterling silver 925; platinum 950 — printed directly via SLM. Surface finish Ra 3–5µm as-printed, mirror polish on request.

💎

End-to-End Production

Full-service from CAD through casting or direct print to setting, stone-setting, polishing, and hallmarking. Prototype to production volumes from one source.

✏️

CAD Design Service

Concept sketch to production-ready CAD file. Parametric models for easy resizing and material variation. Export to STL, STEP, or Rhino format.
