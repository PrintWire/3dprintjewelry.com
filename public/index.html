Jewelry Production Materials

Two routes to a finished jewelry piece — wax-and-cast for maximum material choice, or direct metal print for maximum geometric complexity. Both routes start from your CAD file.

Castable resin with zero-ash burnout. 18K yellow, white, and rose gold via direct SLM. Sterling silver 925, fine silver 999, and platinum 950 on specification.

Jewelry Types

What We Produce

Engagement & Bridal

Bespoke engagement rings and wedding bands with complex stone settings, pavé work, and custom engraving — impossible by hand, perfect by print.

Fashion & Statement Pieces

Architectural forms, interlocking geometries, and lattice structures for contemporary fashion jewelry that pushes beyond traditional casting limits.

Fine & Precious Metal

18K gold, platinum, and sterling silver printed directly via SLM. Skip casting entirely for complex hollow forms and internal channel designs.

Commercial Production

Consistent castable wax patterns at production volumes. Same-geometry repeatability across hundreds of pieces — ideal for collections and catalog runs.

Jewelry Printing Services

Castable Wax Models

Direct Metal Printing
