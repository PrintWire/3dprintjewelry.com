About 3D Print Jewelry

Our Story

Where the Jeweler's Craft Meets Additive Manufacturing

3D Print Jewelry is the authoritative resource for jewelry additive manufacturing — covering castable wax printing, direct precious metal SLM, and the CAD workflows that drive both.

We work with independent jewelers, fashion designers, and commercial jewelry manufacturers to bring complex designs to life without the constraints of traditional lost-wax casting.

From a single bespoke engagement ring to a production run of 500 identical pendants — our printing and finishing capabilities scale to the project.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Design-Led Process

We start with what you want to make, not what our machines can do. CAD consultation, shrinkage compensation, and print parameter optimization all happen before the first layer.

Precision Output

50µm SLA resolution for castable models. Mirror polish to Ra < 0.1µm on direct metal prints. Hallmarked and ready for retail.

Full Production

Wax model to casting to stone-setting to polishing under one roof. Or just the wax — you choose how much of the chain we handle.
